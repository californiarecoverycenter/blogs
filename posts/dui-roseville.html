DUI Program — Roseville

By California Recovery Center — 2026-07-20

This post summarizes key information about the DUI program in Roseville, including program structure, who it serves, enrollment steps, and local resources. For full original details, visit the source: calrecoverycenter.com — DUI Roseville.

Overview

The Roseville DUI program provides court-approved education and treatment services for individuals charged with driving under the influence. Programs typically combine classroom education, counseling, and monitoring to reduce recidivism and support safe driving behaviors.

Program structure

  • Assessment: An initial intake evaluates each participant’s needs and risk level.
  • Education classes: Group sessions covering alcohol/drug effects, decision-making, and legal responsibilities.
  • Counseling / treatment: Individual or group therapy where indicated, tailored to the participant’s clinical needs.
  • Monitoring & compliance: Periodic check-ins and reporting to the court as required.

Who this program is for

The program is intended for individuals with DUI charges or those ordered by the court to complete a rehabilitation/education course. It accommodates a range of risk levels from low to high and offers different intensities of treatment based on assessment.

How to enroll

  1. Contact the program office to request an intake assessment.
  2. Complete required paperwork and provide any court or referral documentation.
  3. Attend the assessment appointment and follow the recommended schedule.
  4. Communicate with your attorney or caseworker to ensure the program satisfies court requirements.

Local logistics

Roseville program locations typically offer daytime and evening class options to accommodate work schedules. Fees, session length, and specific scheduling vary — contact the provider directly for the most accurate information.
